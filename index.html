Hide Scrollbar While Allowing Scrolling

This is a simple demo project where you can scroll through the content without ever seeing the scrollbar. The scrollbar is hidden using CSS, but scrolling is still fully functional using your mouse, trackpad, keyboard arrows, or touchscreen gestures.

Why would you want to hide the scrollbar?

Sometimes designers prefer a cleaner and more minimal look on their websites. Scrollbars can feel distracting in certain layouts, especially if the content area is designed to look like a card or a smooth reading experience. By hiding the scrollbar, we can make the design feel lighter and more focused on the content itself.
